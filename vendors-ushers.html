Ushers

Information

A wedding usher is often the first person to greet guests. A good usher will ease wedding day stress by providing a smooth flow into the ceremony.

  • Greet guests
  • Hand out programs / ensure every chair has a program on it (TBD)
  • Direct guests to seating
  • Ensure front 1st row on the left side and 1st & 2nd rows on the right side are reserved for family (these rows will also have signs)
