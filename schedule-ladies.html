Hair & Makeup Schedule

  • Make sure you hair is clean and DRY the morning of; it's recommended to wash your hair the night before
  • The night before, use a hydrating, lifting and/or radiance mask; skip the cleansing clay mask as they can create a spot or two in the morning
  • Continue your typical skin-care routine; a clean face will hold makeup better
  • It is recommendeded to skip sunscreen and to bring a non-white finishing / setting powder (for touchups) as these will result in a whitecast in photos (also all events are indoors)
  • Breakfast will be served around 7 AM & Lunch will be served at 11:30 AM
  • Anyone with their makeup done before eating will have lip touchups afterwards
