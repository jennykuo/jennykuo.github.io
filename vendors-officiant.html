Info for Vendors

Officiant

Information

We will be having two officiants - a non-religious, close friend of the couple (Dong Kim) and a person of faith (Hannim Kim). Ideally between the two speakers, we craft a ceremony that bridges the importance of faith for the couple and for the non-religious guests.

Dong will be responsible for presiding over the main ceremony events as well as sharing personal anecdotes about the couple and marriage. Hannim will be responsible for the short sermon (5-10 minutes, maximum) and also ask Wesley Yoon to provide a few words of faith / prayer for the couple.

For a full list of ceremony events, timeline, and the responsible speaker, please click here.
